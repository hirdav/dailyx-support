Welcome to DailyX

DailyX is a modern productivity companion built for thinkers, doers, and self-reflectors. It's more than just a to-do list — it’s your daily ritual, journal, and hustle log all in one space.

🌱 Grow Through Your Grind

Whether you’re conquering your goals or documenting your mood, DailyX helps you stay mindful and motivated. With recurring tasks, XP tracking, and streaks, your habits become part of a rewarding journey.

📝 Journaling Made Effortless

Each task is a moment to reflect. Jot down notes as you work, log your wins and struggles, or save thoughts for future you. Your completed tasks hold your entire hustle history.

😌 Track Your Mood

Before or after your day, take a second to check in with yourself. Choose your current mood and optionally leave a journal entry — giving you emotional insight alongside productivity data.

🔥 Built for Consistency

Use recurring tasks to build habits. Earn XP every time you complete something. Watch your streak grow and feel the productivity hit with each completed tasks . It’s simple, gamified, and fun.

🎯 Your Space, Your Flow

No unnecessary clutter. No creepy data collection. Just you, your goals, and the focus to show up daily. Everything is saved locally and privately — because your progress is personal.
